Salted Caramel Pretzel Bark

Sweet and savory, crunchy caramel and chocolate bark

Ingredients

  • 8 oz mini pretzels
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 cups milk chocolate chips
  • sea salt to taste

    • Steps

    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
    2. Line 11x17 pan with aluminum foil
    3. Spread preztels into a single layer on baking sheet
    4. Combine butter and brown sugar in saucepan and cook on medium-low heat until sugar is dissolved and mixture has thickened, about 5 to 8 minutes. Stir often.
    5. Pour mixture over pretzels
    6. Bake for 5 minutes
    7. sprinkle chocolate chips over pretzel and caramel mixture. Cook 1 minute more
    8. While chocolate is warm, spread with spatula evenly over caramel and pretzel mixture
    9. Cool 2 to 3 hours, break into chuncks, and Enjoy!
