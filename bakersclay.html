Bakers Clay

Make homemade ornaments and crafts with this easy to use clay recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 cups salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Mix together flour, water, and salt in large mixing bowl
  3. Knead dough until smooth
  4. On a floured surface, roll out dough until roughly 1/8 inches thick. Use cookie cutters to cut desired shapes. Be sure to use toothpick or other utensil to create a hole at the top to thread ribbon to hang ornament
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour/li>
  6. Cool completely, decorate with paint and preserve with varnish if desired
